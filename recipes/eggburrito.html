Epic Egg Burrito

How to make a delicious breakfast egg burrito

This recipe is a banger of a burrito to start your morning off right. It's simple to make and will be a staple morning delight for the whole family.

all you need is some basic simple ingredients, and some awesome tortilla folding action. Because lets be honest.. no one wants a messy burrito. Just some practice and you'll be making this scrumptious burrito like a pro.

The ingredients to make this epic egg burrito

  • Tortilla (two tortilla's if you're up for the challenge
  • Two medium sized eggs
  • Sour cream (low fat if you're watching your figure)
  • One avacado (the sfuff millenials eat)
  • Thick cut bacon
  • Shredded cheese of your choice (recommended cheddar)
  • Your favorite hot sauce

How to assemble your epic egg burrito

  1. Warm tortilla/s in microwave for twenty seconds.
  2. Lay tortilla flat and spread that creamy goodness that is sour cream first.
  3. Add the two scrambled eggs on top of the sour cream.
  4. Add your bacon, cheese, avacado, and finally that lovely hot sauce.
  5. Now roll that tortilla! Making sure you tuck the edges while rolling. (Don't worry this part takes some practice)
